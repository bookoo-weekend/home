What is this?

Startup Week is a time for everyone to gather, learn, and build a new product in the space of one week. A lot needs to happen to start a startup; a weekend usually isn't enough.

The Bookoo Ventures Startup Week is a Monday through Friday event, where you will gather with your peers, pitch ideas, make a team, build, market, and sell your product all over 5 days. There will also be short trainings every morning by various leaders of industry. Concluding our event, each startup will pitch their company to Bookoo Ventures for an oppurtunity to become a "Bookoo Ventures" company.👏
